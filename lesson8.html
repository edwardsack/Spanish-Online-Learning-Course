Day 8: Irregular Verb "To Be" – Describing What Things Are

Estimated Time: 30 minutes

Objective: Learn the irregular verb to be (ser) to describe identity and characteristics, reinforcing What is that?.

1. Intro Video Placeholder (You)

Explain that to be (ser) is irregular and used for identity (It’s a hotel) and traits (It’s pretty). Connect to What is that? (Day 2). Preview: Describing people and places.

2. Grammar Focus – To Be (Ser)

PronounConjugationExample
YosoyI am a tourist.
TúeresYou are a guide.
Él/EllaesIt’s a hotel.
NosotrossomosWe are tourists.
Ellos/EllassonThey are tickets.

Structure: I am/You are + article + noun (+ adjective). Example: Es una playa bonita.

Who is it? → It’s a waiter.

Key Rule: Ser is irregular (unlike to buy). Use for identity/permanent traits.

Task: Change I am a tourist. to You are a guide.

3. Vocabulary – People/Places

SpanishEnglish
el turistathe tourist
la guíathe guide
el camarerothe waiter
la personathe person

4. Listening Prompt (You Provide)

  • Say: I am a tourist. It’s a pretty beach. They are tickets.
  • Ask: Who is it? → Pause for: It’s a waiter.

Goal: Understand to be and people nouns.

5. Reading + Matching Task

  • It’s a big tourist → The tourist
  • We are guides → We are guides
  • It’s a pretty person → The pretty person

Identify: pronoun, verb, noun in You are a guide.

6. Speaking Prompt

  • Who is it? (point to waiter) → It’s a waiter.
  • Say you’re a tourist → I am a tourist.
  • What is that? (point to beach) → It’s a pretty beach.

7. Writing Practice

  • I am a tourist → Soy turista.
  • It’s a new guide → Es una guía nueva.
  • They are big hotels → Son hoteles grandes.

8. Recap Challenge (Speaking)

  • What is that? → It’s a pretty beach.
  • Say who you are → I am a tourist.
